Privacy Policy — Shadow Seals: Ninja Awakening

Developer: Voidseal Games
Last updated: 2026-07-19

The short version

Shadow Seals: Ninja Awakening is a single-player game with no user accounts. The whole game is playable offline — only the in-app Shop needs an internet connection. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal information, and we never see your payment details. Your save data stays on your device.

Data we collect

None. We run no servers of our own, and the app has no accounts, no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking. All game progress (characters, currencies, settings) is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. The app requests internet access for one purpose only — connecting to Google Play for in-app purchases (see below). Nothing about you is sent to us.

Notifications

The app can show optional local reminders (for example when your idle rewards are full or the daily reset arrives). These are scheduled entirely on your device — no data is sent anywhere. On Android 13+ the app asks for notification permission first, and you can turn reminders off at any time in the in-game Settings or in Android's app settings.

Advertising

The app currently contains no advertising. If optional rewarded ads are ever added in a future update, this policy will be updated before that version is released, and any such ads will always be opt-in.

In-app purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases (gem packs and similar). All purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We — the developer — do not collect, see, or store any payment information: your card, billing address, and transaction details are handled entirely by Google. Google Play returns only a purchase confirmation to the app, and your entitlements (gems, items) are recorded only in your local save. Google's handling of your payment data is governed by Google's own privacy policies. Purchases are entirely optional — the whole game can be played without them.

Children

The game is suitable for a general audience and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Your save data

Deleting the app deletes your save. We have no copy of it and cannot restore it. (Cloud save may be offered in a future update; this policy will be updated first.)

Changes to this policy

Updates will be posted at this page and in the app's store listing before taking effect.
